High Profile Advocacy

Elevating Awareness and Influence
Donation: $0/ $10,000 DONATE NOW

At Child-Help Kenya, we recognize the power of advocacy in driving positive change for individuals affected by Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus. Our High Profile Advocacy cause aims to amplify awareness, influence policy and champion the rights of those living with these conditions in Kenya.

Our advocacy efforts extend beyond raising awareness and influencing policy; they encompass a holistic approach to championing the rights and dignity of individuals affected by these conditions. We strive to create a supportive and inclusive environment where individuals with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus are empowered to thrive and fully participate in society.

By amplifying their voices, advocating for equitable access to healthcare and support services, and challenging societal barriers and discrimination, we work tirelessly to ensure that every individual receives the care, respect, and opportunities they deserve. Here's how we're making a difference:

  • Raising Awareness: Through strategic partnerships and targeted campaigns, we shine a spotlight on Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus, educating the public, policymakers and healthcare professionals about the challenges and needs of individuals living with these conditions. By raising awareness, we aim to reduce stigma, increase understanding, and promote inclusivity.
  • Influencing Policy: We advocate for policies and legislation that prioritize the rights, access to healthcare and support services for individuals with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus. By engaging with policymakers, government agencies and advocacy groups, we work to shape policies that address the unique needs of those we serve and ensure equitable access to care and resources.
  • Empowering Voices: Our High Profile Advocacy initiatives empower individuals affected by Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus to share their stories, experiences and perspectives. By amplifying their voices, we foster a sense of community, solidarity and empowerment, while also challenging stereotypes and misconceptions.
  • Building Partnerships: We collaborate with high-profile individuals, celebrities, influencers and organizations to lend their voices and platforms to our cause. By leveraging their influence and reach, we expand our advocacy efforts, reach new audiences and garner support for our mission.
  • Driving Change: Through targeted advocacy campaigns, events and initiatives, we drive tangible change in policies, practices and attitudes towards Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus. By mobilizing collective action and harnessing the power of advocacy, we strive to create a more inclusive, supportive and equitable society for all.

Our Mandate:

Registered as a nonprofit organization and working in affiliation with Child-Help International our mandate is to foster strategic partnerships to maximize the efficacy and efficiency of service delivery by key partners and prevent duplication of efforts.

We achieve this by focusing on the following key areas:

  1. High Profile Advocacy and Awareness.
  2. Resource Mobilization.
  3. Monitoring and Evaluation.
