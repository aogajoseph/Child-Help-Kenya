Our Mandate:

Registered as a nonprofit organization and working in affiliation with Child-Help International our mandate is to foster strategic partnerships to maximize the efficacy and efficiency of service delivery by key partners and prevent duplication of efforts.

We achieve this by focusing on the following key areas:

  1. High Profile Advocacy and Awareness.
  2. Resource Mobilization.
  3. Monitoring and Evaluation.
