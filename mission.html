Our Mission & Vision

  • Return Home
  • Mission/Vision

We are on a mission

To enable a generation where children with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus in Kenya, become thriving adults in an inclusive society by working with parties worldwide.

Mobilizing Resources

Our mission is to raise funds and allocate resources, working with parties worldwide, to support initiatives that benefit children with these conditions in Kenya.

Coordinating Support

We are creating a supportive community to bring together all stakeholders involved in caring for and serving individuals with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus in Kenya.

Raising Awareness

We are spreading awareness on these conditions, to increase knowledge, research and understanding and encourage acceptance and inclusion in Kenya and globally.

High Profile Advocacy

We advocate for improved access to better healthcare, education and opportunities to ensure children with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus can participate fully in society.

Measuring Impact

Our Monitoring and Evaluation criteria help us assess the effectiveness of our initiatives, adjust strategies accordingly and improve outcomes for affected individuals.

Fostering Independence

By encouraging skill development and independence-building activities we empower children with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus to lead autonomous lives.

Our Vision

By the year 2050, all children with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus in Kenya get a fair start.

Early Detection and Intervention

We implement widespread screening programs and access to prenatal care to identify and address Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus in infancy, ensuring early intervention and treatment.

Access to Comprehensive Healthcare

Through our specialized pediatric clinics and outreach partners across Kenya we provide holistic medical care, surgical interventions and rehabilitation services for children with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus.

Youth Empowerment

We support inclusive skill development and talent nurturing practices and provide mentorship, resources and accommodations to ensure children with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus have equal access to opportunities for personal success.

Community Support and Capacity Building

We have developed community-based support networks and empowerment programs to provide emotional support, practical assistance and advocacy resources for families raising children with Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus, fostering a supportive and inclusive environment for all.

We are a Consortium in Kenya, that creates a supportive environment to bring together all stakeholders involved in caring for and serving individuals affected by Spina bifida and Hydrocephalus.

Our Mandate:

Registered as a nonprofit organization and working in affiliation with Child-Help International our mandate is to foster strategic partnerships to maximize the efficacy and efficiency of service delivery by key partners and prevent duplication of efforts.

We achieve this by focusing on the following key areas:

  1. High Profile Advocacy and Awareness.
  2. Resource Mobilization.
  3. Monitoring and Evaluation.
